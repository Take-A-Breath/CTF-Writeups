```
bandit11@bandit:~$ echo "Gur cnffjbeq vf 5Gr8L4qetPEsPk8htqjhRK8XSP6x2RHh" | tr '[A-Za-z]' '[N-ZA-Mn-za-m]'
The password is 5Te8Y4drgCRfCx8ugdwuEX8KFC6k2EUu
```
